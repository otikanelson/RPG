About

Shadow of the Arcane is a text-based RPG built with vanilla JavaScript — a dark fantasy adventure where your choices shape the story.

Explore a world of arcane mystery, turn-based combat, immersive sound, and branching narrative paths as you guide Eliza through the shadows.

This project started as a way to learn JavaScript while building something I could actually play — and it grew into a full interactive experience.
